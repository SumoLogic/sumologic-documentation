We’re excited to introduce advanced filtering capabilities on the **OpenTelemetry Collection** page in Sumo Logic, allowing you to quickly locate relevant OpenTelemetry collectors using filters such as **Collector Type**, **Collector Tag**, **Status**, and **Upgrade Available**.

These filters help you narrow down collectors more efficiently, reduce time spent searching through large catalogs, and simplify day-to-day collection management. [Learn more](/docs/send-data/opentelemetry-collector/view-opentelemetry-collection-page/#find-the-opentelemetry-collectors-using-filters).
