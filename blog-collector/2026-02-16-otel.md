We are excited to announce that we’ve added powerful filtering capabilities to the Sumo Logic Source Templates page, allowing you to quickly find relevant source templates using filters such as **Type**, **Status**, **Created By**, and **Collector Tags**. These filters make it easier to narrow down templates based on your needs, reduce time spent searching through large catalogs, and streamline day-to-day source template management. [Learn more](/docs/send-data/opentelemetry-collector/remote-management/source-templates/manage-source-templates/#find-source-templates-using-filters).
