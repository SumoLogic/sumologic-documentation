We're happy to announce that you can now track trace data volume alongside logs and metrics for your Source Templates and OpenTelemetry Collectors. This makes it easier to spot when a source template or collector isn't sending data and to monitor how much data it's ingesting, reducing undetected data gaps and giving you clearer, easier-to-read visibility into your data collection health. [Learn more](/docs/send-data/opentelemetry-collector/view-opentelemetry-collection-page).
