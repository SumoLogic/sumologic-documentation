In this release, we've added **Winget** (Windows Package Manager) support for uninstalling and upgrading the OpenTelemetry Collector on Windows. This streamlines lifecycle management on supported systems while maintaining full compatibility with existing installation methods. [Learn more](/docs/send-data/opentelemetry-collector/install-collector/windows/#uninstall).
