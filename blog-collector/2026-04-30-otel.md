We’re excited to introduce the **Status Overview** panels on the OpenTelemetry collection page in Sumo Logic. These panels provide a centralized, high-level health snapshot of OpenTelemetry collectors, highlighting key metrics such as offline collectors, collectors pending upgrades, and remotely managed collectors without any configured sources.

Each panel is interactive and you can click them to drill down into filtered lists for fast diagnosis and resolution. [Learn more](/docs/send-data/opentelemetry-collector/view-opentelemetry-collection-page/#status-overview).
