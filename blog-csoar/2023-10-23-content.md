This release introduces several new integrations, including **Atlassian Confluence** and **Google Drive**, alongside
various integrations that have been migrated and are now accessible through App Central.  
We've also improved multiple
integrations to leverage the new Cloud SOAR API, introduced new actions, and implemented various general fixes and
enhancements.

* [New] Google Drive
* [New]* Cofense

* [New]* AbuseIPDB
* [New]* APIVoid
* [New]* VMware Carbon Black Cloud Endpoint Standard
* [New]* VMware Carbon Black Cloud Enterprise EDR
* [New]* VMware Carbon Black Cloud Platform
* [New]* Lacework
* [New]* Sumo Logic
* [New]* Sumo Logic Notifications
* [Updated] CSE Tools
