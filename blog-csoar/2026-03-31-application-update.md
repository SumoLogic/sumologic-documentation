## Cloud SOAR and the Automation Service are FedRAMP compliant

We are happy to announce that [Cloud SOAR](/docs/cloud-soar/) and the [Automation Service](/docs/platform-services/automation-service/) are now FedRAMP compiant. You can use these features with confidence that they adhere to the strict demands of FedRAMP. 

Sumo Logic offers a brand range of features that meet the requirements of FedRAMP compliance. [Learn more](/docs/manage/manage-subscription/fedramp-capabilities/).
