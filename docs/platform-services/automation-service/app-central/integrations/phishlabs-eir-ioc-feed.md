PhishLabs by Fortra is a cyber threat intelligence company that delivers Digital Risk Protection through curated threat intelligence and complete mitigation. PhishLabs provides brand impersonation, account takeover, data leakage and social media threat protection.

## Actions

* **List Incident Indicators** *(Enrichment)* - Retrieve list of incidents and indicators within the feed
* **List Global Indicators** *(Enrichment)* - Retrieve global list of indicators

## PhishLabs EIR - IOC Feed in Automation Service and Cloud SOAR

1. To configure the integration, log into the application, expand the configuration menu in the top right corner by hovering over the gear icon and click Automation. <br/>![](/img/platform-services/automation-service/app-central/integrations/phishlabs-eir-ioc-feed/phishlabs-eir-ioc-feed-1.png)

1. In the Automation section, on the left menu, click Integrations. <br/>![](/img/platform-services/automation-service/app-central/integrations/phishlabs-eir-ioc-feed/phishlabs-eir-ioc-feed-2.png)

1. After the list of the integrations appears, search for the integration and click on the row.

1. The integration details will appear. Click on the "+" button to add new Resource. <br/>![](/img/platform-services/automation-service/app-central/integrations/phishlabs-eir-ioc-feed/phishlabs-eir-ioc-feed-3.png)

1. Populate all the required fields (\*) and then click Save.
   * Label: The desired name for the resource
   * URL: Your PhishLabs EIR - IOC Feed API URL
   * Username: Your PhishLabs EIR - IOC Feed Username
   * Password: Your PhishLabs EIR - IOC Feed API Password <br/>![](/img/platform-services/automation-service/app-central/integrations/phishlabs-eir-ioc-feed/phishlabs-eir-ioc-feed-4.png) <br/>![](/img/platform-services/automation-service/app-central/integrations/phishlabs-eir-ioc-feed/phishlabs-eir-ioc-feed-5.png)

1. Click Test Saved Settings. <br/>![](/img/platform-services/automation-service/app-central/integrations/phishlabs-eir-ioc-feed/phishlabs-eir-ioc-feed-6.png)

1. You should receive a successful notification in the bottom right corner. <br/>![](/img/platform-services/automation-service/app-central/integrations/phishlabs-eir-ioc-feed/phishlabs-eir-ioc-feed-7.png)
