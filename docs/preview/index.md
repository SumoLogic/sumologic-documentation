Sumo Logic uses a standardized Customer Preview program to bring customers into early access for new products and features before they reach general availability (GA). Each preview tier reflects the maturity of the feature and the scope of customer participation.

| Release type | Who can participate | For production use | Interface stable | SLA | Support | Product maturity |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **Private Preview** | Invite only | No | No | No | Product team | 60–75% |
| **Extended Preview** | Selected customers | No | No | No | Product team | 75–90% |
| **Public Preview** | All eligible customers | Yes | Yes | Yes | Support team | 90–100% |
| **General Availability** | All customers | Yes | Yes | Yes | Support team | 100% |

## Private Preview

Private Preview features are available to a small, curated group of customers recruited by the product team. This tier is used to validate features with design partners before broader rollout.

- Participation is by invitation only.
- Features are 60–75% production-ready.
- The interface and behavior may change.
- No SLA applies.
- Documentation may be limited or unavailable.

## Extended Preview

Extended Preview features are available to a selected group of customers who meet eligibility criteria. Your account team nominates you for participation, and the product team reviews and approves nominations.

- Participation requires nomination by your account team.
- Features are 75–90% production-ready.
- The interface and behavior may change.
- No SLA applies.
- Documentation is available but may be incomplete.

## Public Preview

Public Preview features are available to the broadest group of customers that meet eligibility criteria. Features at this tier are production-ready and stable.

- Participation is open to all eligible customers.
- Features are 90–100% production-ready.
- The interface is stable.
- SLA applies.
- Full documentation is available.

## General Availability

Generally available features are fully supported and production-ready for all customers. No special enrollment is required.

:::note
During the transition from Beta to Customer Preview, you may see both labels active at the same time. Full adoption of the Preview program takes effect at launch.
:::

Preview (formerly _Beta_) features are capabilities that are coming soon to general availability. To participate, contact your Sumo account executive.

Previews are different than our generally available services in that they have additional terms and conditions for participation:
* We may make available to you a Preview, Limited Release, Alpha, Beta, or other pre-release version of the service, applications, or APIs for non-production use.
* Previews may have limited features, functions, indexing capacity, storage, data security, data continuity, data retention or other limitations as determined by Sumo Logic.
* Sumo Logic may discontinue the Preview at any time.
* We may also decide never to make the features and functionality in Preview generally available.
* Previews (by their nature) have not been fully tested as they are still under development, and may be inoperable or incomplete, including more errors and bugs than our generally available offerings.
* Previews are offered “as is” with no warranties or indemnities.
