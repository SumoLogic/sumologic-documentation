### New release

- **Amazon Bedrock AgentCore**. The Sumo Logic Amazon Bedrock AgentCore app provides preconfigured dashboards that deliver visibility into Amazon Bedrock AgentCore logs and performance metrics, covering agent runtimes, memory, gateways, built-in tools, and identity services, to help you monitor and optimize your AI agent deployments. [Learn more](/docs/integrations/amazon-aws/amazon-bedrock-agentcore/).
