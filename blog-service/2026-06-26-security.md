We're excited to announce that we have redesigned the Threat Intelligence source configuration experience. Threat Intelligence is now accessible under **Data Management > Threat Intelligence**, making it easier to discover and manage your threat intel sources.

Key improvements include:

- A new **Add Source** flow that lets you select from available threat intelligence sources, choose a collector, and complete configuration in a streamlined workflow.
- A new option to **manually upload indicators** with a downloadable template for the required file schema.
- An updated **Sources listing page** showing source name, last successful poll, type (static or dynamic), health status, and number of indicators.

[Learn more](/docs/security/threat-intelligence/).
