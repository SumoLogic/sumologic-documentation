We are excited to introduce the Metrics Browser, an interactive catalog that lets you quickly search and explore all available metrics, dimensions, and dimension values in your environment, without running a query.

Metrics Browser simplifies the entire metrics workflow by:
* Providing an intuitive way to discover what metrics exist and how they are structured.
* Showing metric names alongside their associated dimensions and values.
* Making it easy to select metrics and add them directly into metrics queries.

Metrics Browser significantly reduces time spent on exploratory queries, accelerates troubleshooting, improves accuracy of investigations, and lowers data consumption and cost.

[Learn more](/docs/metrics/metrics-queries/metrics-explorer/#metrics-browser).
