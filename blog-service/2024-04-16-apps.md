We're excited to announce an update of all our terraform based monitors. Below are the list of enhancements that were a part of this update.

- Upgraded AWS and Sumologic provider versions for CVE fixes.
- Fixed minor bugs.

For details on upgrade instructions, refer to the [changelog](https://github.com/SumoLogic/terraform-sumologic-sumo-logic-monitor/releases).
