The **Go To** search bar now searches across a broader set of content in addition to navigation pages:

- **Library content**. Search dashboards, searches, and folders saved in your Library.
- **Monitors**. Find monitors by name directly from the search bar.
- **SLOs**. Locate SLOs without navigating to the SLO page.
- **No-match behavior**. If no results meet the relevance threshold, the search returns 0 results instead of showing unrelated suggestions.
