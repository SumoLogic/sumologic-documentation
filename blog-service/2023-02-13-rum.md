We've added the ability to filter Real User Monitoring (RUM) dashboards by deployment environment. To enable this, you'll need to add the `deployment.environment` tag and value that corresponds to your development environment (like `us-west-1`, `prod`, `dev`) to your RUM script.

Learn more:
* [RUM Dashboards](https://help.sumologic.com/docs/apm/real-user-monitoring/dashboards)
* [Configuring RUM Data Collection](https://help.sumologic.com/docs/apm/real-user-monitoring/configure-data-collection)
