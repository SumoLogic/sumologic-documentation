We’re excited to introduce the new Sumo Logic app for Anthropic Compliance. This app delivers centralized visibility into Anthropic platform activities, including API usage, authentication, integrations, billing, user lifecycle events, and organizational changes. It enables security, compliance, and operations teams to detect threats, investigate suspicious behavior, and maintain governance through real-time dashboards, analytics, and event correlation across Anthropic environments. [Learn more](/docs/integrations/saas-cloud/anthropic-compliance).
