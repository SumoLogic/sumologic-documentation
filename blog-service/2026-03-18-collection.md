We’re excited to introduce the new Sumo Logic source for Proofpoint TRAP, enabling seamless ingestion of message logs via the Proofpoint TRAP API. This integration provides enhanced visibility into email threats by delivering detailed insights on message disposition, detected threats, and policy actions. Security teams can now more efficiently identify, investigate, and respond to email-based attacks with improved context and analysis.
