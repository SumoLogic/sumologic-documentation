New - we've released an OpenTelemetry Kubernetes Operator for tracing instrumentation. When you enable and install this operator, your application is instrumented automatically and traces will be sent to Sumo Logic. [Learn more](https://help.sumologic.com/docs/apm/traces/get-started-transaction-tracing/opentelemetry-instrumentation/kubernetes).
