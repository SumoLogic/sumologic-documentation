We're excited to announce the release of 36 new setup guides to our App Catalog, enabling you to connect with various GCP services to Sumo Logic, which allows you to collect and analyze the data. These setup guides provide easy step-by-step configuration instructions for setting up the data collection and exploring your data in Sumo Logic.
