We are happy to announce that you can now forward raw log data from Sumo Logic to an S3 destination. Previously, you could only forward metadata. You can now choose to forward only log data, log data and metadata, or log data with metadata and enriched fields, in either CSV or JSON format.

[Learn more](/docs/manage/data-forwarding/amazon-s3-bucket#configure-an-s3-data-forwarding-destination).
