We're excited to introduce the new Sumo Logic app for Data Volume for MSSP. This app provides detailed insights into data usage across child organizations, segmented by data type, tier, category, collector, source, and host. It leverages predefined dashboards to help MSSPs efficiently monitor and analyze ingest volume across their managed environments. [Learn more](/docs/integrations/sumo-apps/data-volume-mssp/).
