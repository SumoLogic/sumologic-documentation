We're excited to introduce the new Sumo Logic app for Varonis. This app provides detailed insights into data usage across child organizations, segmented by data type, tier, category, collector, source, and host. It leverages predefined dashboards and searches to help MSSPs efficiently monitor and analyze ingest volume across their managed environments. [Learn more](/docs/integrations/sumo-apps/data-volume-mssp/).
