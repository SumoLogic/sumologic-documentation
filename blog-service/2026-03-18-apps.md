- **Data Volume app for MSSP**. We’re excited to introduce the new Sumo Logic Data Volume app for MSSP, delivering detailed insights into data usage across all child orgs. With rich segmentation by data type, tier, category, collector, source, and host, the app enables precise analysis of ingest patterns at every level. Leveraging predefined dashboards it empowers MSSPs to efficiently monitor, analyze, and optimize ingest volume across their entire managed environment. [Learn more](/docs/integrations/sumo-apps/data-volume-mssp/).
- **Netskope**. We’ve added new [monitor alerts](/docs/integrations/security-threat-detection/netskope/#netskope-alerts) and the following dashboards to enhance visibility into administrative activity and insider risk within your Netskope environment:
  - [Admin Audit & Compliance](/docs/integrations/security-threat-detection/netskope/#admin-audit--compliance)
  - [Watchlist & Insider Threat](/docs/integrations/security-threat-detection/netskope/#watchlist--insider-threat)
