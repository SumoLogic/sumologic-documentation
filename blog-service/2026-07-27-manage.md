We're excited to introduce the **Manage Features** capability for all customers. With this, you can opt in or opt out of specific Sumo Logic features for your organization directly from the Organizations UI without needing to raise a request with Sumo Logic support. [Learn more](/docs/manage/manage-subscription/create-and-manage-orgs/manage-org-settings/#manage-features).
