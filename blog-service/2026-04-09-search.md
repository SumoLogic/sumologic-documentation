We’re excited to introduce in-app tabs to our **Search** page, making it easier to manage multiple searches within a single browser window. With in-app tabs enabled, you can reorder tabs using drag-and-drop and scroll horizontally for smoother navigation, helping you move between searches more efficiently. [Learn more](/docs/search/get-started-with-search/search-page/).
