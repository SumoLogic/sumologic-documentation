We're excited to announce the release of our new Azure Event Hubs source for logs collection. This source collects [Resource Logs](https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/azure-monitor/platform/resource-logs-schema) and [Activity Logs](https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/azure-monitor/platform/activity-log-schema) with greater ingestion speed and scalability. [Learn more](/docs/send-data/collect-from-other-data-sources/azure-monitoring/ms-azure-event-hubs-source/).
