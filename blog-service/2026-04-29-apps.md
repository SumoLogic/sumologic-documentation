We’re excited to introduce the following new Sumo Logic apps:

- **LiteLLM**. The Sumo Logic app for LiteLLM provides preconfigured dashboards and alerts to monitor usage, cost, performance, and infrastructure for reliable LLM proxy operations. [Learn more](/docs/integrations/saas-cloud/litellm).
- **Upwind**. The Sumo Logic app for Upwind helps you send threat, configuration, and audit events to Sumo Logic via webhook for centralized monitoring. [Learn more](/docs/integrations/webhooks/upwind/).
