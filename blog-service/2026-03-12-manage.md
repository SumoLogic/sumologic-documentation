We are excited to introduce the ability to manage lookup tables within library content on our **Manage Content** tab. With this enhancement, MSSP (which typically have a parent organization with multiple child organizations) administrators can conveniently push lookup table items in the Library folder to multiple child organizations at once, simplifying content distribution and management. [Learn more](/docs/manage/manage-subscription/create-and-manage-orgs/manage-orgs-for-mssps/).
