We are pleased to announce the availability of the [Sumo Logic App for CyberArk EPM](https://help.sumologic.com/docs/send-data/hosted-collectors/cloud-to-cloud-integration-framework/cyberark-source). This new cloud-to-cloud connector uses Cyberark APIs to retrieve administrative audit events from every Set in your CyberArk environment.
