We are excited to announce that when you install a Next-Gen app, you will be subscribed to receive email notifications whenever an upgrade is available.

Each notification includes:
- The app name and new version.
- Release notes detailing what has changed.
- A direct link to the app in the App Catalog.

Additionally:
- You can unsubscribe at any time by navigating to the app within the Sumo Logic App Catalog.
- Uninstalling an app automatically removes all upgrade notifications for it.
- Users with the Manage Apps capability can subscribe or unsubscribe from notifications for apps installed by others.

[Learn more](/docs/get-started/apps-integrations).
