We are excited to announce that when you install a Next-Gen app, you are now automatically subscribed to receive email notifications when an upgrade becomes available. The notification includes the app name, new version, release notes, and a direct link to the app in the App Catalog. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in the notification email. Uninstalling an app automatically removes all upgrade notifications for it. Users with Manage Apps capabilities can also subscribe or unsubscribe from apps installed by others. [Learn more](/docs/get-started/apps-integrations).
