We're excited to announce the ability to manage your **Library** content across your Enterprise multi-orgs and MSSP (typically a parent organization with multiple child organizations). This lets you to save, share, and manage log searches, dashboards, apps, and other Sumo Logic content for launching searches and dashboards with a single click.
