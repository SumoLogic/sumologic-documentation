We're excited to introduce support for filtering the scheduled dashboard reports by child orgs. When setting up a scheduled report, select the required child org(s) from the **Organization** dropdown under the **Select Variables** section to receive a report that reflects data for your intended child organization. [Learn more](/docs/dashboards/dashboard-child-orgs/#create-scheduled-reports).
