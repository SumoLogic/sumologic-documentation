We're introducing **Customer Preview**, a new standardized program that replaces Beta for early access to new Sumo Logic products and features. Customer Preview brings consistent naming, clearer participation criteria, and more structured feedback across all early access programs.

There are three Preview tiers:

* **Private Preview**. A small, curated group of customers validates features with the product team. Features are 60-75% production-ready.
* **Extended Preview**. A broader selected group provides scaled feedback. Features are 75-90% production-ready.
* **Public Preview**. The widest eligible audience gets early access to features that are 90-100% production-ready.

During the transition period, you may see both Beta and Preview programs active at the same time. Full adoption of the Preview program takes effect at launch.
