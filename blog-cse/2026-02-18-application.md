### Bulk update insights

We're happy to announce that you can use the UI or API to update multiple insights at a time, including closing, reassigning, adding comments, or giving them a new status. Acting on multiple insights at once speeds up your insight resolution. [Learn more](/docs/cse/get-started-with-cloud-siem/about-cse-insight-ui/#bulk-update-insights).
