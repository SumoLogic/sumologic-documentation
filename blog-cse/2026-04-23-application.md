## Sigma rules support

We are excited to announce that you can now write detection rules once in [Sigma](https://sigmahq.io/) and convert them to Sumo Logic Cloud SIEM rule syntax through a Sigma CLI plugin. This eliminates the manual effort of rewriting your Sigma rules and accelerates the migration of existing Sigma rule libraries to Cloud SIEM. The translated rules detect the same threats and behaviors as the original Sigma rules. [Learn more](/docs/cse/rules/convert-sigma-rules/).
